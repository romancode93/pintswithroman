Since graduating high school, I noticed life started moving at a pace. Everyone is engrossed with their own set of problems and invested in their own tailored dreams. To the point where it becomes difficult to make time for friends and family.
However, it is just as important that we spend that odd leisure hour here and there to unwind and maintain some semblence of mental health by rewarding ourselves.
With that purpose in mind, I created Pints with Roman, a place to honor social commitments.
This is a platform which allows me to keep my friends and family informed about any life updates and indulge them in the proverbial Pint Talk!
'Tis quite simple! Through the contact me link at the bottom of the page, you can contact me and leave your details and preferred date & time. We can exchange dialogue over emails and agree on a preferred platform to video call (I would recommend Zoom!) and set up a mutually agreed apointment.
The appointment does not have to be long but I do suggest you do yourself the courtesy to set-aside atleast a quarter-hour to relax and indulge in a fine drink with your pal. Now the premise is Pints and I suggest we each treat ourselves to the amber elixir, but you may choose to indulge in any other choice-drink. While the premise remains the drink(I do suggest you indulge in the odd glass of alcohol but coffee does the trick just as well), I want you to utilize this time and social-appointment to divulge and absorb information about you & me. You may wish to indulge in any life updates, discuss any life-choices you seek to make, discuss a hobby, a sport, or simply discuss a friend or mutual personality. The point is to have a place that allows you to check-out of your busy life and take a breath. Think of it as a breather. Indulge in a smoke if that's what it takes for you to relax, but make time for the casual. Feel like you are being heard and be a social being. Indulge in some Pint talk. There was a time when a lot of you did confide in me, I just want you to know that I am here if you wanna get a word out.
